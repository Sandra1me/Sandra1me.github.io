A LITTLE ABOUT ME!

HEY THERE!
My name is Sandra, and if you didn't notice, I like animals a lot! Let me tell you who I am:I'm an industrial technology engineer specializing in mechanical engineering. Currently, I'm also on the way to finishing my electronics and automatics master's degree, which will help me become a mechatronics engineer.I have a degree in industrial engineering (focused on mechanical engineering) and a master's degree in CAD, FEM and simulation with CATIA V5 and SolidWorks.I'm also improving my programming skills by practising and taking different courses. Right now, I'm focused on developing my knowledge of MATLAB and Python by taking various lessons and doing diverse projects.- - - - -I have more than four years of experience in mechanical engineering, having worked for the aerospace, automotive and wind industries. During those years, I was responsible for making different designs and structural analyses with CATIA V5 and Hypermesh, both for the aerospace and automotive industry. Also, in one of my jobs, I was part of the quality team, in charge of homologating different parts.Also, I've been working as a freelance translator and mechanical design engineer for some months.I'm currently working for the wind industry as a systems engineer, supporting factories and wind farms and designing different wind farms.- - - - -MY SKILLS
- Design: AutoCAD, CATIA V5, NX
- Unigraphics, SolidWorks
- Rendering: CATIA Composer, Keyshot
- Structural Analysis: CATIA V5, SolidWorks- Programming Languages: MATLAB, Python
- Code Editor: Spyder, Visual Studio Code
- Version Control: Git, GitHub- - - - -If you want to see some of my projects and work, do not hesitate and visit my PORTFOLIO!You can check my 3D models in my Behance profile or my coding projects on GitHub.
